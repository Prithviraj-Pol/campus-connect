# 🚀 CampusSync

> *A Multi-Tenant, Role-Based College Event & Venue Management SaaS*

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

**What problem are we solving?**

College events are often managed independently by isolated departments, leading to frustrating venue scheduling conflicts, poor cross-department coordination, and reduced student participation. Furthermore, tracking post-event attendance and managing multiple college branches on a single unified platform is a costly, manual logistical nightmare for administration.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**How does our project solve this problem?**

CampusSync is a centralized platform designed to streamline the entire event lifecycle across multiple college campuses securely. 

* **Smart Booking:** Real-time conflict detection prevents HODs from double-booking venues.
* **Priority Overrides:** Admins can resolve clashes by overriding standard events for high-priority institutional needs.
* **Personalized Feeds:** Students see an event dashboard filtered by their specific interests (e.g., Tech, Cultural) and their specific college.
* **Live Attendance Tracking:** HODs can delegate a "Student Coordinator" who uses a mobile-responsive dashboard to check registered students in at the door, providing instant analytics.
